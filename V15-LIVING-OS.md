# BLACKTERM OS v15 — Living World Update

Adds a cinematic boot, local observer memory, progressive clearance, procedural system events, and seasonal environment effects.

## Included
- Cinematic city boot sequence
- Returning-visitor greetings stored locally in the browser
- Clearance progression based on visits and explored apps
- Procedural event feed with active-app reactions
- Summer heat shimmer, October ghost protocol, and winter snow
- Version labels updated to v15.0

Global online observer counts are intentionally not simulated. That feature requires a real backend or analytics endpoint.
